Description

It's time We delve into this delightful junk food tangle seasoned with our finest cherry picked ingredents. You won't want to miss it

Ok now it's time to listen what we've used to transform an other wise bland meal into a delacacy

ingredents

  • Black Pepper
  • A pinch of salt
  • Jamaican Grace Cock Soup
  • All Purpose and Jerk Seasoning
  • Egg(optional)
  • Ramen Bag contents

Steps

  1. Put water on heat unti it starts to boil

    2. Keep in mind if you are including eggs to have them boil for atleast 10 minutes before throwing in any additional ingredients(or youcan boil them in a separate container

  2. Season Boiling Water

    3. It is imperative you season the water first before adding the noodles. This includes the black pepper, salt, ramen packet, jerk and all purpose seasoning.

  3. Throw in Ramen and let it boil for two minutes and done!
